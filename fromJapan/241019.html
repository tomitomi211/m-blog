'The Rum Diary' (2011) is a film based on the novel of the same name by Hunter S. Thompson.

The protagonist, journalist Paul Kemp (Johnny Depp), moves from New York to Puerto Rico,

where he begins working as a newspaper reporter.

He meets American entrepreneur Sanderson (Aaron Eckhart) and his fiancée Chenault (Amber Heard),

and gradually gets drawn into a complicated relationship and a shady business scheme.

As for my thoughts...

It was more of a comedy than I expected!

I thought it would be all about serious journalism chasing facts,

but the movie had a much lighter, more upbeat tone.

That said, Amber Heard was stunning! I couldn't help but be captivated during her scenes.
